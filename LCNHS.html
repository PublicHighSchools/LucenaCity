LUCENA CITY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

School Head : MR. RODOLFO A. SENA JR.

Brgy. Ibabang, Lucena City Quezon 4301

Lucena City National High School started as Ibabang Dupay National High School (IDNHS) located at Ibabang Dupay Lucena City. It was founded on July 16, 1993 through Republic Act 1192. It was initiated by Dr. Leonora Guerrero, Division Superintendent with the help of Dr. Azucena Romulo, Assistant Schools Division Superintendent, Mrs.Evelyn Villabrosa, Elvira Razon Aranilla Elementary School (ERAES) Principal,formerly Ibabang Dupay Elementary School Mr. Ricardo Orinday, North District Supervisor,and Mr. Rolando Ingles, Parent-Teachers Association (PTA) President. During the school year, Dr. Guerrero wanted to change the school’s name as Science National High School but the students’ average did not meet the required grade standard for Science Curriculum.
